Portal Introduction

Clean Pak Portal is a municipal complaint management system designed to help citizens report sanitation issues, track complaints, and improve city cleanliness through digital services.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to create a cleaner and healthier city through technology, transparency, and citizen participation. Our vision is to build a smart municipal system where complaints are resolved efficiently and services are accessible online.

Authority Information

This portal operates under the Municipal Corporation and Waste Management Authority. It supports government initiatives for smart city management and sanitation improvement programs.

Policies & Guidelines

• Citizens must provide accurate complaint information
• False reports may be rejected
• Complaints are processed according to municipal policies
• Data privacy is maintained under government regulations
